Behold yon stars, eternal fires so bright, 
Their whispers sing of God’s almighty hand. 
The heavens vast, where wonders greet the night, 
Reveal His craft, divine and ever grand.

The moon on seas its silver glow doth cast, 
A dance unseen, yet felt by hearts that pine. 
In hidden realms where cosmos dreams are massed, 
What truths await, what glories so divine?

This universe, a tapestry well wrought, 
Each mote of dust by unseen forces bound. 
Though mortal ken may grasp but fleeting thought, 
Its beauty speaks where holy truths are found.

Through veils of mystery, our souls do yearn, 
To seek His will, and heaven’s path discern.
